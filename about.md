

# About Me

My name is Quinn Doyle, I am a Master's in Public Policy student pursuing a career in housing and land development. My long term goals are to contribute to economic development in Philadalphia through an intersection of community and environmental development; often focusing on intentional, place-based investments in affordable housing, community gardens, and more.

 **Pronouns:** She/Her

- **Current role:** Master's in Public Policy Student

- **Interests:** Web mapping, spatial analysis, land use, creative geography display

-  **Goals:** I am interested in deploying spatial analysis and mapping to highlight areas prone to economic disinvestment and highlight need for reinvestment efforts. This may include analyzing and displaying socioeconomic factors, concentration of businesses, vacancy prevalance, and more. I am always looking for new ideas on how to approach economic development issues within Philadelphia, including geography display policy analysis, and reccomendations
