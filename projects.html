Philadelphia Healthy Food Store Site Suitability

This project identified potential areas for a new healthy-food store in Philadelphia. The analysis considered empowerment zones, access to subway and regional rail stations, and distance from existing farmers markets and Healthy Corner Store Initiative locations.

Methods: Buffer, dissolve, union, erase, intersect, area calculation, and site suitability analysis.

Chicago Crime and Airbnb Analysis

This project examined homicide density and Airbnb activity by Chicago police district. The analysis combined point data, district boundaries, summary tables, spatial joins, and area calculations.

Methods: Select by attribute, spatial join, summary statistics, joins, field calculations, and choropleth mapping.

Flint Water Infrastructure Risk

This project evaluated which Flint wards faced the greatest potential risks from lead service lines, nearby toxic-release facilities, and water pollution. The final analysis identified areas where multiple risk indicators overlapped.

Methods: Coordinate-system transformation, buffering, overlay analysis, and ward-level risk assessment.

Demographic Patterns Across the United States

This project used Census data to map the percentage and total population of Hispanic residents by state. It compared choropleth and proportional-symbol mapping methods.

Methods: Census data joins, field calculations, classification, choropleth mapping, and proportional symbols.
