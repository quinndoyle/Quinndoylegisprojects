  Chicago Crime &amp; Economic Development

<h2 class="section-heading">
  Recommendations for a City-Supported Grocery Store
</h2>

<p>
This Philadelphia site-suitability project identifies areas that may be
eligible for a city-supported grocery store. The analysis considers
empowerment zones, access to subway and regional rail stations, and distance
from existing farmers markets and Healthy Corner Store Initiative locations.
</p>

<h2 class="section-heading">
  Lead Service Line Risk in Flint
</h2>

<p>
This project evaluates areas of Flint that may face heightened drinking-water
risks. The analysis considers lead service line materials, ward boundaries,
water pollution, and nearby Toxic Release Inventory facilities.
</p>
